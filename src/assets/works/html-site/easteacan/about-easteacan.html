ABOUT US

EasTeaCan is an Alternative Rock band from Tehran, Iran formed in 2013 by Mani Tabibzadeh (Vocals, Guitar) , Siavash Sojoudy (Keyboards) and later joined by Masoud Sheikhi (Drums) and Mehrdad Hamedi (Bass). Their first album (released in the summer of 2015 and simply titled “EasTeaCan”) is the result of their Collaborations through this two years. After the release of the album the band went through some member changes. Mahan and Ali left the band and Mehrdad Hamedi (Bass) joined as a Replacement for Ali Sayah. Long may they reign the kingdoms of Rock n’ Roll.

We like to thanks to Amir Khalaj, Arash Pakzad, Siamak Mohebalian, Fariba Moezi, Hadi Jamali, Peyman Kerman, Ali Zarasvand, Mani Monadizadeh, Nusha Zamani, Saba Jazayeri, Raffik Barseqian, Mana Arian, Aileen Asatouri and Shadi Tabibzadeh for their help with making this album and their contributions to the band.

About EasTeaCan

EasTeaCan is an Alternative Rock band from Tehran, Iran formed in 2013 by Mani Tabibzadeh (Vocals, Guitar) , Siavash Sojoudy (Keyboards), Mahan Tabrizinia (Guitars), and later joined by Masoud Sheikhi (Drums) and Ali Sayah (Bass). Their first album (released in the summer of 2015 and simply titled “EasTeaCan”) is the result of their Collaborations through this two years.
